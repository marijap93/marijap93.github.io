Key Values

Reducing clothing waste

By buying second-hand we are reducing our waste and we make a difference on our consumption. Also, in this way clothes and accessories get a second chance.

Exposing timeless art to the world

Now we get a chance to add more colors to the world by wearing art! Some of the most famous paintings throughout history can be a part of your outfit. Be unique by wearing one-of-a-kind piece of clothing.

Providing help where it is needed

You are officially helping someone by purchasing anything from the shop. I will donate the money to carefully chosen organizations or individuals where I think they will really make a difference. I will make sure to be transparent and share everything and you will be a big part of the story!
